Valcrim

Martian Agility Recon Scout (MARS) Research Project

Designing flight where it shouldn't exist

Mission Statement

Valcrim aims to develop a next-generation Martian rotorcraft platform capable of sustained, energy-efficient aerial operation in low-density atmospheric conditions.

The mission is to extend capabilities of planetary exploration by enabling long-duration reconnaissance, high-resolution terrain mapping, and environmental monitoring, while supporting future robotic and human exploration of Mars.
